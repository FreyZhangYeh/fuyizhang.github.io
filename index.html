I am a second-year undergraduate student at Zhejiang University, with internship experience at Alibaba Group. My primary research and engineering interests include Large Language Models (LLMs), Multi-Agent Systems, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI-Generated Content (AIGC), and Autonomous Driving.

  Projects

Prism

GitHub

Prism is a deep research system powered by large language models, designed to help users distill valuable insights from vast amounts of information through multi-round, progressive inquiry. The system is built on an agentic architecture, featuring a comprehensive memory hierarchy, dynamic planning capabilities, and real-time monitoring.
